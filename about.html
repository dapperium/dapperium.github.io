About Sumit Jangra

Who am I?

I’m Sumit Jangra, a 20-year-old fashion content creator from Haryana and the face behind Dapperium. I blend minimal fashion with cinematic frames and modern tech to craft clean, sharp, aesthetic visual stories.

Why Dapperium?

Dapperium isn’t just a name — it’s my evolving pehchaan. A blend of “Dapper” and “Premium,” it reflects my style: minimal, cinematic, and aesthetically sharp.

Vision Ahead

Dapperium is growing into more than a page — it’s the seed of a future fashion label + creative studio..
